Start-from-a-template gallery

The gallery UI, grid, and card renderer already ship (LLD 43) and are unchanged by this task. The design work is the 3 new catalog-showcase plans and their hand-authored inline-SVG thumbnails, which must read as part of the existing set. This mockup renders the real gallery panel in the app's dark theme so the new thumbnails (marked new) can be judged against the four originals.

Thumbnail language (kept): 120×90 viewBox, gold room outline on a faint tint, furniture as gold-filled rects/circles, ~0.18 fill for solid pieces and ~0.10 for surfaces. Rugs render as a dashed-border tinted rectangle beneath the furniture.
