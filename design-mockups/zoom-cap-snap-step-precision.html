Finer snap for in-room placement

Today the smallest positional step is ~0.25 m (~0.8 ft): the grid step is chosen adaptively from zoom (finest NICE_STEP whose on-screen spacing ≥ 56 px), and the MAX_ZOOM = 8 cap makes the 0.1 m grid unreachable. Placement snaps to that same grid, so you can't nudge furniture by an inch.
