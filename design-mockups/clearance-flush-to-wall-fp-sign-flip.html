Clearance — flush-to-wall verdict

When a symbol is seated flush against a wall face (the LLD 26 "seat against the wall" gesture), the fit verdict must read the same on every wall. Today a floating-point sign flip at gap = 0 makes the right wall say "Won't fit" while the top wall says "0.0m fits." These mockups show the fix and a copy decision.
