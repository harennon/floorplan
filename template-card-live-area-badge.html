Template card — live area badge

Each card's floor area is computed live from its own plan via polygonArea summed over rooms — the exact same source the HUD reads. Toggle units to confirm the badge tracks metric/imperial. Note the "1-bedroom" card: its old copy said "~55 m²" but the plan actually measures 52.50 m² — the drift this change removes.

Units
Option A — Pill in the name row

A compact gold pill sits to the right of the template name. Reads as a spec chip, keeps the thumbnail clean, and lives in the existing flow (no absolute positioning). Recommended: lowest footprint, clearest "this is a fact about the plan" framing.

Option B — Corner overlay on thumbnail

The area floats over the top-right of the preview, like a photo timestamp. Most eye-catching and ties the number to the drawing, but overlaps thumbnail content and needs a scrim for contrast.

Option C — Metric footer row

A bordered footer beneath the description presents the area as a labelled figure, echoing the measurements panel. Most room to grow (could add perimeter later) but the tallest card.
